# NSG - Project Context

## Philosophy

The purpose of NSG is not purely revenue generation, and it is not about creating slop with AI or helping others create AI slop. The purpose is to help people carrying out important work do their important work more effectively.

NSG's aim is not to impose our worldview or methodology onto clients. It is to listen to their needs and build something that gets them where they need to be. We are not project management consultants. We do not dress things up in consulting lingo. We listen, we build, we hand it over.

There's nothing more permanent than a temporary solution that works. In the same vein, if it doesn't work, it doesn't work. We will simply work until the client says "yes, this works."

Our clientele is likely quite non-technical - public employees, brick-and-mortar store owners, school administrators, teachers. NSG's work is aimed at people for whom SEO is not central to their work. The eventual hand-off is intended to work seamlessly for clients after our work together is done. If we have to come back to polish anything that was detailed in a previous SOW, that work is free - unless the client previously expressed contentment with it and now wants a cosmetic change.

We take a human approach to our work. We meet in person to get to know each other and discuss the issues at hand. Then when we get to work, we always have a mutual understanding of the work to be done and keep the client or prospective user in the feedback loop throughout the development process to ensure it meets their needs. We value simplicity in our work.

## Tone & Language

- Write for non-technical business owners (think: dispensary owner, not developer).
- Avoid jargon: no "sprints," "runways," "deliverables," "clean code," "full-stack," "frameworks," "browser," etc.
- Be confident and direct, not corporate or boutique-precious.
- When talking about AI, be practical and grounded - never preachy or utopian.
- Red is an accent color only - never overwhelming.
- White space is your friend.
